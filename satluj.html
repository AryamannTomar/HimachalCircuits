Satluj Circuit
A: SHIMLA – CHAIL – NALDEHRA – PARWANOO – SWARGHAT – BILASPUR – BAROG – KASAULI – PAONTA SAHIB – RENUKAJI

B: NARKANDA – RAMPUR – SARAHAN – ROHRU – KHARAPATHAR


The Sutlej Circuit derives its name from the ancient river Sutlej. It passes through snow covered majestic Himalayas, forests of pine, oak and deodar, across stone fruit and apple orchards and through the Shiwalik foot hills.


This circuit covers:
Delhi – Parwanoo – Kasauli- Barog – Solan – Chail – Hatkoti – Rampur – Sarahan – Narkanda – Naldehra – Tattapani – Shimla – Kiarighat – Delhi. This circuit also includes Renuka, Paonta Sahib and Nahan.
